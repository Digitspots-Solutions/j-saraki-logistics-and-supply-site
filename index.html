About Us

NJD Jay Solar Solutions Limited (also known as NJD Jay Off-Grid Power Co) is a leading renewable energy company strategically located in Eleme Local Government Area, Rivers State. Positioned at the heart of Nigeria's oil and gas corridor at Indorama Petrochemical Model Trailer Park, Refinery Road, Eleme, we understand the unique power challenges facing Nigerian industries and businesses.

Led by experienced renewable energy strategists, we specialize in designing, installing, and maintaining cutting-edge off-grid solar power systems that guarantee uninterrupted energy supply. Our solutions eliminate dependence on unreliable grid power and expensive diesel generators, offering sustainable, cost-effective alternatives for factories, warehouses, office complexes, hotels, hospitals, and residential estates.

With deep expertise in both the oil & gas sector and renewable energy technology, we deliver world-class power solutions tailored to Nigeria's demanding operational environment.
