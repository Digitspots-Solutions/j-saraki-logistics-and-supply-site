About Us

J Saraki Logistics and Supply is a leading provider of logistics and supply chain solutions serving the manufacturing sector in Eleme and across Rivers State. Strategically positioned at the Indorama Petrochemical Model Trailer Park along Refinery Road, we leverage our prime location to deliver timely, efficient, and cost-effective services to manufacturing plants, petrochemical facilities, and industrial operations throughout the region. Our commitment to reliability, safety, and operational excellence has made us the preferred logistics partner for businesses requiring consistent, professional support services.
